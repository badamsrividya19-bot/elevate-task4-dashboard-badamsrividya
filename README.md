2. Project Overview

Task 4 focuses on analyzing sales behavior, profit patterns, customer segments, product categories, and regional performance using a clean e-commerce dataset.
The dashboard was designed to present:

Monthly sales trends

Category-wise and subcategory-wise contribution

Profitability and discount impact

Regional and segment performance

Customer purchasing behavior

All insights are supported with visual charts and backed by data extracted from the structured dataset.

4. Key Insights from the Dashboard
1. Monthly Sales Trend

Sales gradually increase with seasonal variations.

October–December shows the highest revenue contribution.

2. Category Performance

Electronics and Home categories lead overall sales.

Fashion categories show moderate but stable contribution.

3. Regional Performance

West and South regions drive the majority of total sales.

North region displays strong order volume but lower profit margins.

4. Discount Impact

Excessive discounts negatively affect profit consistency.

Balanced discount strategy improves revenue retention.

5. Customer Segment Insights

“High-Value” and “Frequent Buyer” segments contribute a major share of total revenue.

New customers show high conversion potential.
